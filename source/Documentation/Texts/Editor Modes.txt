You can change what mode you're in with the three buttons on the top of the screen.

Object Mode:
You can place and move obejcts in obejct mode.
You can select what object to place from the Templates panel on the left side of the screen.

Vertex Mode:
Vertex mode is only available for draggable obejcts (Surface, PlayerMover).
You can move the vertices of the object in this mode.
LeftClick on vertices to select/deselect them.
Vertices become Green when selected and become Yellow when not selected.
Use WASDEQ to move the selected vertices based on the Compass.

Variable Mode:
You can change the variables of objects in this mode.
